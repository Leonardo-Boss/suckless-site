dmenu
=====
dmenu is a dynamic menu for X, originally designed for
[dwm](//dwm.suckless.org/). It manages large numbers of user-defined menu items
efficiently.

--------
* [dmenu-4.8](//dl.suckless.org/tools/dmenu-4.8.tar.gz) (2018-03-14)

Development
-----------
