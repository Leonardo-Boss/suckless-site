ubase
=====
ubase is a collection of tools similar in spirit to util-linux but
much simpler.

The following programs are currently implemented:

* chvt
* clear
* ctrlaltdel
* dd
* df
* dmesg
* eject
* fallocate
* free
* freeramdisk
* fsfreeze
* getty
* halt
* hwclock
* id
* insmod
* killall5
* login
* lsmod
* lsusb
* mknod
* mkswap
* mount
* mountpoint
* pagesize
* passwd
* pidof
* pivot_root
* ps
* respawn
* rmmod
* stat
* su
* swapoff
* swapon
* switch_root
* sysctl
* truncate
* umount
* unshare
* uptime
* watch
* who
