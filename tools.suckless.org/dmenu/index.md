DMENU
=====

dmenu is a dynamic menu for X, originally designed for
[dwm](http://dwm.suckless.org/). It manages large numbers of user-defined menu
items efficiently.

--------

* [dmenu-4.2](http://dl.suckless.org/tools/dmenu-4.2.tar.gz) (20101119)
